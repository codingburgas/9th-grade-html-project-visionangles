The Northwestern Bulgaria Firefighting Team is a dedicated unit committed to protecting lives, property, and the environment across the Vidin, Montana, and Vratsa regions. With courage, professionalism, and strong community ties, our firefighters respond swiftly to emergencies and work tirelessly to ensure public safety. United by duty, we serve with honor every day.

  • Water Cannon System: High-pressure suppression from a safe distance.

Personal Equipment

Helmet

Heat-resistant composite shell with an integrated visor and torch mount for visibility in dense smoke or darkness.

SCBA Air Pack

Self-contained breathing apparatus supplying clean oxygen during operations in toxic or smoke-filled environments.

Protective Gloves

Heat-insulated gloves with flexible grip, designed for both safety and dexterity during rescue tasks.

Radio Communicator

Rugged, long-range radio unit allowing clear communication with dispatch and other responders in real time.

Rescue Tools

Thermal Imaging Camera

Detects body heat through smoke, helping firefighters locate trapped individuals quickly and accurately.

Halligan Bar

A versatile tool for prying doors, forcing entry, or breaching walls during emergency rescues.

Hydraulic Cutter

Powerful device used to slice through metal during vehicle extractions or collapsed structures.

Portable Ladder

Collapsible, lightweight ladder providing quick access to upper floors, rooftops, or windows.

Fire Alert Portal

Protecting communities through early fire detection and rapid response coordination. Together, we can save lives and preserve what matters most.
